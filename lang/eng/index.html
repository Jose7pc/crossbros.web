What is Popol?

The video gaming revolution

Have you ever had an idea to create a video game but you didn’t know how to carry it out? At that moment, you have realised about all the elements you need to accomplish it: a lot of resources, knowledge of coding and tons of time. And these difficulties are common in every video game’s company: from the biggest one, to the smallest one. Popol wants to solve this problem.


Popol has a very large library of resources, made by people that, as same as you, want to share their work. All this is accessible to anyone who join the community and, that way, we will be able to create the biggest library of resources that have ever existed. Finally, all the work that has been used and has produced money, will get their share!

CREATE IT

Share your artistic flair and create everything that you have ever dreamt of. Each and every work is welcome at Popol.

SHARE IT

Contribute in other users’ projects by sharing your ideas and all your work.

PLAY IT

Design your own video game supporting you in the community or enjoy our directory of video games done by other users.

EARN MONEY

Bring onto the market your video games or earn money with the work you have uploaded if they help others.

What does it offer?

Now that you are aware of all the things you can do, we want to show you how. Popol is going to stand on three basics.

Content creators community

Content creators of all kinds will meet at Popol, putting together a lot of resources and a great variety of knowledge.

Free catalogue of resources

Download the community’s resources and use them in Popol for free.

Graphics engine

Import the resources from the web to the foolproof and powerful graphics engine. Ready to create your video games.
